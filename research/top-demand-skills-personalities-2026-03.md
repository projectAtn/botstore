# BotStore demand research (2026-03)

## Method
- Used direct-source fetches from:
  - OpenAI GPT Store launch page (evidence of category demand + builder activity)
  - a16z Top 100 GenAI consumer apps (evidence of engagement patterns and category growth)
  - Anthropic news stream (evidence that providers are prioritizing agents/professional workflows)
- Note: broad search API is currently unavailable in this runtime, so this report combines fetched signals + product reasoning.

## Key market signals
1. **Builder supply is huge**: OpenAI reported millions of custom GPTs built quickly after launch.
2. **Discovery categories that recur**: writing, research, programming, education, lifestyle.
3. **Consumer pull remains strong for creative tooling** (a16z trend snapshots).
4. **Provider strategy is moving toward agents/professional work**, not only chat.

## Top 10 high-demand SKILL packs (ranked)
1. **Inbox + Calendar Operator**
   - Why: universal pain, clear ROI in hours saved.
2. **Research Analyst (source-grounded)**
   - Why: high demand from students, academics, analysts, founders.
3. **Coding Copilot (repo-aware)**
   - Why: sticky daily use and willingness to pay.
4. **Content Studio (multi-format drafting + editing)**
   - Why: aligns with persistent writing/creative demand.
5. **Meeting Intelligence (prep, notes, follow-ups, CRM sync)**
   - Why: immediate business utility.
6. **Sales Assistant (prospecting + personalized outreach + CRM updates)**
   - Why: direct revenue linkage.
7. **Data Ops Analyst (Sheets/SQL/BI actions + reports)**
   - Why: broad B2B relevance.
8. **Customer Support Triage + Resolution Agent**
   - Why: operational cost savings at scale.
9. **Personal Admin / Life Ops (travel, bills, reminders, forms)**
   - Why: high consumer retention when integrated deeply.
10. **Automation Builder (cross-tool workflow orchestration)**
   - Why: power users and teams create compounding value.

## Top 10 high-demand PERSONALITY packs (ranked)
1. **Executive Chief of Staff** (concise, proactive, high-accountability)
2. **Socratic Tutor** (adaptive teaching, exam mode)
3. **Therapist-lite Coach** (supportive but bounded; no medical claims)
4. **Founder Strategy Partner** (decision framing, prioritization)
5. **Creator/Brand Voice Editor** (consistent style and audience fit)
6. **Debate/Red Team Persona** (argument stress testing)
7. **Warm Companion Persona** (low-friction daily engagement)
8. **Mythic Mentor Persona** (e.g., Gilgamesh; identity-rich companion)
9. **No-BS Operations Commander** (direct, action-first accountability)
10. **Local Culture/Language Persona** (regional tone and idiom fidelity)

## Why these are hard to replace by base providers
A plain model can imitate style. It cannot easily clone:
- deep workflow integration,
- connector network,
- approval/safety policy behavior,
- organization-specific memory schemas,
- measurable reliability and QA history.

## Product implication for BotStore
Prioritize packs that combine:
- **high-frequency use** (daily/weekly)
- **real-world writes** (calendar/email/tasks/CRM)
- **identity lock-in** (personality users keep)
- **proof of outcomes** (success/error/retention metrics)
